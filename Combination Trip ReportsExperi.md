# Combination Trip Reports/Experience Reports 
#### Guidelines: 

*   Combination substances only considered a substance if it has been made official (ex. Dramamine, adderall etc.), or when a substance has been widely recognized and integrated (ex. Ayahuasca, Changa etc.) 
*   Combination substance are separate when they are mainly unofficial and/or have little history & recognition (ex. nootropic stacks, herbal & recreational blends, ‘flipping’ experiences etc.) 
*   A substance that which is naturally occurring as one body but contains a set of constituents (ex. Alkaloids, terpenoids etc) is still considered as one substance 
*   Psychoactive and non-psychoactive substances are considered and to be explored
*   A set of common substances are to be exempt from a combination where there is no intention of additional psychoactive effect, where no significant additional effects are reported, when the dosage is low and/or the ingestion is outside of the main experience timeframe, temporary - select pre-2025 reports may also be exempt (exempt combinations will be placed in the main [Trip Reports](Trip%20ReportsExperience%20Reports.md) section with corresponding color codes):
    *   Cannabis
    *   Alcohol
    *   Nicotine
    *   Tobacco
    *   Caffeine 
*   Miscellaneous low-impact substances are to be exempt from a combination where there is no intention of additional psychoactive effect, where no significant additional effects are reported, when the dosage is low and/or the ingestion is outside of the main experience timeframe, temporary - select pre-2025 reports may also be exempt (exempt combinations will be placed in the main [Trip Reports](Trip%20ReportsExperience%20Reports.md) section with corresponding color codes):
    *   Example
